How to use RSoft

If you want to try some simulations of waveguides, special structure fibers or some other optical components, the RSoft may be one of the tools that you can’t miss.

The RSfoft is a combine of several subsoftwares such as RSoft CAD environment, FullWAVE, BeamPROP, DiffractMOD, ModePROP, GratingMOD, BandSOLVE, in which the bolded ones are perhaps the mostly used in our labratory.

Official Documents

Thera are several useful official documents, which you can download from the following links or copy from the server ++in our student room++ directly.

  • rsoftcad.pdf (telling you how to draw components and set parameters), and Japanese version rsoft_cad_manual_v90_jpn.pdf.
  • beamprop.chm
  • fullwave.chm
